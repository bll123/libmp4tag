### Tag Names

Cover image tags.  Cover tags may specify the cover image index and
also a cover name.

'covr' and 'covr:0' are the same tag for the first cover image.
<br>'covr:0:name' is the name of the first cover image.
<br>'covr:1' is the second cover image.
<br>'covr:1:name' is the name of the second cover image.

Custom tag names.  Custom tags have a tag name with three parts.
'----', the application-name, and the name of the tag.  e.g.
'----:BDJ4:DANCE' and
'----:com.apple.iTunes:MusicBrainz&nbsp;Track&nbsp;Id' are custom
tags.
