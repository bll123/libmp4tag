### Getting Tags

##### mp4tag_duration

Returns the duration in milliseconds.

~~~
#include <stdint.h>

int64_t mp4tag_duration (libmp4tag_t *libmp4tag)
~~~

**libmp4tag** : The libmp4tag_t structure returned from mp4tag_open.

Returns: duration in milliseconds or 0.
